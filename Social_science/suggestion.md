Suggestions for Final Examination

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Course Code: SS-1106

Course Title: Government and Public Administration

**Define state. Briefly discuss the elements of states. Differentiate between state and government. Discuss the types of governments. Which governmental system do you prefer and why—explain. Describe the govt. three organs. Write the functions of all organs.**

**Define Constitution, Law, Act, Fundamental Rights. Write down the classifications of constitution. Describe the characteristics of a suitable constitution for any country. Explain the nature of constitution of Bangladesh. Distinguish between human rights and fundamental rights. Describe the main fundamental rights of Bangladesh constitution. What obstacles do citizens face in ensuring fundamental rights?**

**What is local government and classify it. Discuss the characteristics and advantages of local government in Bangladesh. Differentiate between local government and local self-government. Evaluate the upazila system as a unit of local government. Write down the functions of UNO.**

**Define democracy. Differentiate between democracy and autocracy. Discuss the preconditions of democracy. Write down the merits and demerits of democracy. Mention the importance of free and fair election for a democratic country.**

**What is bureaucracy? Write down the characteristics of bureaucracy. Discuss the role of bureaucracy in policy formation in Bangladesh. What is meant by accountability and transparency? Discuss the problems to achieve accountability and transparency in administration.**

**Define public administration. Discuss the similarities and dissimilarities between public and private administration. Write down the importance of study of the public administration.**

**Define good governance. Write down the elements of good governance. How good governance can be established in a developing country like Bangladesh. Briefly discuss the drawbacks of ensuring good governance.**

**Discuss the administrative system of Bangladesh. What is administrative decentralization. Write down the advantages and disadvantages of administrative decentralization.**

**How can Bangladesh government arrange resource management and planning.**
