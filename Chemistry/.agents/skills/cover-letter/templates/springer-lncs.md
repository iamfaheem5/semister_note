# Springer LNCS / LNAI Cover Letter

> Snapshot for Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science / Artificial Intelligence and related Springer journals (Information Systems, Constraints, ...).

## Tone

- Methodologically focused, reasonably formal.
- Springer editors often handle multi-disciplinary submissions; explain context in one sentence.

## Opening

- First sentence states the problem and the proposed approach.
- Name the manuscript type and target LNCS volume / journal title.

## Contribution Framing

- Three contributions in narrative form (not enumerated).
- Connect each contribution to the manuscript's section structure.
- Quantitative anchor for the headline result.

## Journal Fit

- Two sentences connecting the work to the LNCS volume editor's call for papers, or to the Springer journal's scope.

## Declarations (Required for Springer)

- Originality and exclusivity.
- Competing interests.
- Funding sources (Springer requests this explicitly for many journals).

## Length

- Hard ceiling: 450 words.

## Common Desk-Reject Triggers

- Multi-disciplinary submission without explaining how the work fits the target volume.
- Missing funding disclosure.
