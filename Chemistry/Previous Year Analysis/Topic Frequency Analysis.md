## Study Tips Based on Frequency

1. **Atomic Structure** is the absolute king — almost every paper has 3–4 sub-questions. Prioritize: quantum numbers, electronic configuration, Heisenberg principle, 4s vs 3d, Bohr model.
2. **Chemical Equilibrium** is equally heavy in Finals. Master: Kp/Kc relation, Le Chatelier, derivations with α, numerical on PCl₅ dissociation.
3. **Chemical Bonding + Acids/Bases + Kinetics** form the next tier. Learn derivations by heart (rate constant, half-life, buffer mechanism, hybridization diagrams).
4. **Electrochemistry** has a signature repeating numerical: **EMF of Zn-Ag concentration cell**. This exact question appears in 2021, 2023, and 2024_1st Finals.
5. **Colligative Properties** and **Phase Rule** are smaller but predictable. Raoult's law and Gibbs phase rule appear almost every alternate year.
