### Define Reflexive, Symmetric, Transitive + Check on A = {1,2,3,4}

**2019 Q4(a,b):**
> (a) Define reflexive, symmetric and transitive relation.  
> (b) Consider the following five relations on the set A = {1, 2, 3, 4}: R = {(1,1), (1,2), (2,3), (1,3), (4,4)}; S = {(1,1), (1,2), (2,1), (2,2), (3,3), (4,4)}; T = {(1,3), (2,1)}; U = A × A, the universal relation; V = ∅, the empty relation. Determine whether or not each of the above relations on A is i) Reflexive ii) Symmetric iii) Transitive.

**2021 Q6(a):**
> Consider the following five relations on the set A = {1, 2, 3, 4}: R, S, T, U, V. Determine whether or not each of the above relations on A is i) Reflexive ii) Symmetric iii) Transitive.

**2022 Q3(a,b):**

**2024 Q6(b,c):**

### Composite Relations
> **Years:** 2018, 2019, 2020, 2022, 2023

**2018 Q5(e):**
> What is the composite of the relations R and S where R is the relation from {1,2,3} to {1,2,3,4} and S is the relation from {1,2,3,4} to {0,1,2}.

**2019 Q5(c):**
> f, g, h are functions on X = {1, 2, 3} as given. Compute i) f ∘ g ∘ h ii) f ∘ h ∘ g.

**2020 Q3(d):**
> Let A = {1,2,3,4}, B = {4,5,6} and C = {6,7,8}. R is a relation from A to B and S is a relation from B to C, which is given by R and S. Determine R and S.

**2022 Q7(c,d):**
> (c) Let A = {1,2,3,4}, B = {4,5,6} and C = {6,7,8}; R is a relation from A to B and S is a relation from B to C. Determine R and S.  
> (d) Let A = {1,2,3}, B = {a, b, c} and C = {x, y, z}; Consider the following relations R and S from A to B and from B to C, respectively. Find the composition relation R∘S with arrow diagram.

## Study Notes
- **Always memorize** the exact reflexive/symmetric/transitive check on A = {1,2,3,4}. This set appears every single year.
- Practice drawing digraphs from relation sets and vice versa.
- Hasse diagrams for "divides" relation on {1,2,3,4,6,8,12} is a repeated pattern.
- Relation composition with sets A→B→C is very common.
- Know how to compute R² (matrix multiplication for relations).
