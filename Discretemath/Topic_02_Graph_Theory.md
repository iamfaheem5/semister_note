## Study Notes
- **Graph definitions with figures** is a guaranteed question type. Memorize and be able to draw: Graph, Subgraph, Bipartite graph, Complete graph, Directed graph, Tree, Binary Tree.
- **Isomorphism** appears every other year. Practice drawing two clearly isomorphic graphs.
- **Adjacency matrix ↔ Graph drawing** is extremely common. Practice both directions.
- **Euler path/circuit** is classic — know the conditions (all vertices even degree for circuit, exactly two odd for path).
- **Handshaking theorem** appeared in 2023. Know Σdeg(v) = 2|E| for undirected, and Σin-deg = Σout-deg = |E| for directed.
- **Euler's formula V - E + R = 2** for planar graphs appeared in 2023 — know proof outline.
- **Prim's algorithm** appeared in 2024 for minimum spanning tree. Practice step-by-step.
