## Study Notes
- This topic area was nearly absent before 2023 but had a **massive spike in 2024** with an entire question (Q3) containing 5 sub-parts.
- **Pigeonhole principle** is now a recurring favorite. The exact "three born in same month" problem has appeared twice with identical wording.
- **MISSISSIPPI permutations** tests factorials with repeated elements: 11! / (4!·4!·2!) = 34,650.
- **Officer elections** are simple permutations P(n,r) = n!/(n-r)!.
- **Dice probability** — 6/36 = 1/6 for sum of 7 (most probable sum with two dice).
- **Binomial coefficient** C(7,4) = 35.
- Expect this topic to continue growing in importance in future exams.
