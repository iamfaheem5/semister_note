# CVPR Cover Letter

> CVPR and sister vision venues (ICCV, ECCV, WACV) share most cover-letter expectations.

## Tone

- Empirically focused. Vision reviewers expect strong benchmark evidence.
- Concise; do not pad with motivation already present in the abstract.

## Opening

- One sentence stating the technical contribution (architecture, training paradigm, dataset, benchmark).

## Contribution Framing

- Three concise contributions.
- One quantitative anchor (typically a benchmark result with the dataset named).
- Acknowledge the comparison set briefly.

## Journal Fit

- Two sentences connecting the contribution to the CVPR scope.

## Declarations (Required for CVPR)

- Originality and dual-submission compliance.
- Anonymized submission confirmation (CVPR uses double-blind review).

## Length

- Hard ceiling: 400 words.

## Common Desk-Reject Triggers

- Anonymization violation in the manuscript that bleeds into the cover letter.
- Benchmark results without naming the dataset, split, or protocol.
