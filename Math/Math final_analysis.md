### 1. Beta & Gamma (The "Free 15 Marks" Pack)
**A. Prove β(m,n) = Γ(m)Γ(n) / Γ(m+n)**  
*(Final 2021: 7m, Final 2022: 6m, Final 2024(1st): 6m, Faridpur 2025: 4m, Shyamoli 2025: 5m)*

**B. Prove Γ(1/2) = √π**  
*(Final 2023: 3m, Final 2024: 3m, Faridpur 2025: 3m, NITER 2025: 6m)*

**C. Evaluate Γ(3/2), Γ(5/2)**  
*(Incourse 2025, MEC 2025: 3m)*  
→ Just know: Γ(3/2) = √π/2, Γ(5/2) = 3√π/4.

**D. Evaluate using Beta/Gamma:**  
- ∫₀^(π/2) sin⁵x cos⁴x dx *(NITER 2025: 6m)*  
- ∫₀^(π/2) sin⁵x dx *(Incourse/MEC 2025: 3m)*  
- ∫₀^(π/2) cos⁷x dx by Wallis *(MEC 2025: 3m)*

**E. Prove the Wallis formula:**  
∫₀^(π/2) sinᵖθ cosᵠθ dθ = [Γ((p+1)/2) Γ((q+1)/2)] / [2Γ((p+q+2)/2)]  
*(Final 2021: 6m, Final 2022: 6m, Final 2024: 6m)*

### 2. Maxima & Minima (The "Algorithm" Pack)
**A. The EXACT same function:**  
`f(x) = x³ - 9x² + 15x - 3`  
Find critical points, max, min. *(Final 2021: 7m, Final 2022: 4m, Final 2024(1st): 4m)*  
→ **Algorithm:** f′(x)=0 → 3x²-18x+15=0 → x=1, 5.  
→ f″(x)=6x-18. f″(1)=-12 (Max, value=4). f″(5)=12 (Min, value=-28).

**B. The EXACT profit problem:**  
Sell at Tk. 12, Cost C(x)=20+0.6x+0.01x². Find max profit.  
*(Incourse 2 & 3 2024: 3m & 2m)*  
→ Profit P(x)=11.4x-20-0.01x². P′(x)=0 → x=570.

**C. Lagrange-type:**  
`u = 4/x + 36/y` subject to `x+y=2`. *(Incourse 2/3 2024, Final 2024: 5m)*

### 3. L’Hospital’s Rule (The "Copy-Paste" Pack)
**State the rule** *(1 mark)* — appears in every paper.

**Memorize these exact evaluations:**
1. **limₓ→₀ [1/x - log(1+x)/x²]**  
   *(Final 2022, Shyamoli 2025, NITER 2025)* → Combine to [x-log(1+x)]/x². Answer: **1/2**.
2. **limₓ→₀ (x - tan x)/x³**  
   *(Final 2021, 2023, Shyamoli 2025, NITER 2025)* → Apply L’H 3 times. Answer: **-1/3**.
3. **limₓ→₀ (eˣ - eˢⁱⁿˣ)/(x - sin x)**  
   *(Final 2024)* → Apply L’H once. Answer: **1**.

### 4. Piecewise Functions (The "Template" Pack)
**The "Famous" Function:**  
`f(x) = { 1 (x<0), 1+sin x (0≤x<π/2), 2+(x-π/2)² (x≥π/2) }`

- **Continuity at x=π/2:** LHL=2, RHL=2, f(π/2)=2. **Continuous.**  
- **Differentiability at x=0:** LHD=0, RHD=cos(0)=1. **Not differentiable.**

*(Final 2021: 6m, Final 2023: 6m, Incourse 1 2024: 5m, Incourse 3 2024: 3m)*

**Another exact template:**  
`φ(x) = { x² (x<1), 2.5 (x=1), x²+2 (x>1) }`  
Does lim x→1 exist? **No.** LHL=1, RHL=3.  
*(Incourse 1 & 3 2024)*

### 5. Definite Integrals (The "By-Heart" Pack)
**A. The "Golden Integral"**  
`∫₀^π x sin x / (1+cos²x) dx`  
*(Final 2021, 2023, 2024(1st), Faridpur 2025)*  
→ Use property: **∫₀^π x f(sin x) dx = π/2 ∫₀^π f(sin x) dx**.  
→ Let cos x = t. Answer: **π²/4**.

**B. The "King's Property" Integral**  
`∫₀^(π/2) √sin x / (√sin x + √cos x) dx`  
*(Final 2021, 2022)* → Answer: **π/4**.

**C. Easy substitution:**  
`∫₀¹ x³ √(1+3x⁴) dx` *(Final 2021, 2024(1st))* → Let u=1+3x⁴.

**D. Gaussian:**  
`∫₀^∞ e^(-x²) dx = √π/2` *(Incourse/MEC 2025)*.
